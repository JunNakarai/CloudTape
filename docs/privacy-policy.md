# CloudTape Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-18

CloudTape is a minimal music player for audio files you keep in iCloud Drive or the Files app. The app is designed for private listening and does not collect personal data.

## Data Collection

CloudTape does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

CloudTape does not use:

- Analytics SDKs
- Advertising SDKs
- Tracking technologies
- Third-party account systems
- Remote recommendation services

## Music Files

CloudTape plays music files selected by you from iCloud Drive or the Files app. Files are processed locally on your device using Apple's media playback frameworks.

CloudTape does not upload your music files to any CloudTape server. CloudTape does not operate a server for storing, analyzing, or sharing your library.

If your files are stored in iCloud Drive, syncing and storage are handled by Apple through your iCloud account and device settings.

## Folder Access

When you choose a music folder, CloudTape stores a local security-scoped bookmark so it can reopen that folder later. This bookmark stays on your device and is used only to restore access to the folder you selected.

## Network Access

CloudTape does not make network requests for analytics, advertising, tracking, accounts, or recommendations.

iCloud Drive file availability and downloads are handled by the operating system.

## Children

CloudTape is not designed to collect information from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with the app's release materials.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support link listed on the App Store page.
