CloudTape

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Overview

CloudTape is designed as a private, local-first music player for audio files selected from iCloud Drive or the iOS Files app.

Data Collection

CloudTape does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, account systems, or third-party tracking. The app does not sell personal data.

File Access

CloudTape only accesses folders and files that you choose through iOS document picker permissions. The app may store a security-scoped bookmark so it can reopen the selected folder later.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Support page for contact details.
