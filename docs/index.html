CloudTape

A simple iOS player for the music files you already own.

CloudTape plays audio from iCloud Drive and the Files app with a native, fast, and private iOS experience. It is made for personal music libraries, not subscriptions, feeds, or recommendation systems.

Features

Built for a personal library.

iCloud Drive first

Choose a folder from iCloud Drive or Files and keep your music where you already manage it.

Native playback

Uses iOS media controls, background audio, lock screen controls, and a focused SwiftUI interface.

Fast and quiet

No accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no heavy web stack. Just your files and playback controls.
