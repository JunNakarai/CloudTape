## Description
CloudTape is a minimal music player for your own audio files in iCloud Drive.

Choose a folder, press play, and listen. CloudTape focuses on fast access, natural shuffle playback, and a calm interface that stays out of the way.

It is designed for people who already have music files and want a simple personal listening space without accounts, ads, tracking, social features, or recommendation algorithms.

Features:
- Play audio files from iCloud Drive or the Files app
- Start shuffle playback quickly
- Browse and search your selected folder
- Continue playback in the background
- Use Lock Screen, Control Center, and headphone controls
- Enjoy a lightweight mini player and spacious Now Playing view
- Keep your music local and private

CloudTape is intentionally simple. It is not a streaming service, playlist platform, or music management system. It is a quiet place to play the music files you already own.
